WHAT IS STEM?

Science. Technology.
Engineering. Mathematics.

STEM is not just a set of subjects — it is a way of thinking. It is the foundation upon which every modern career, every breakthrough technology, and every future innovation is built.

S

Science

Understanding the natural world through observation, experimentation and evidence.

T

Technology

Building tools and systems that solve real problems and power the digital world.

E

Engineering

Designing, creating and improving structures, machines and systems that shape our world.

M

Mathematics

The universal language of logic, patterns, and precision that underpins all other STEM fields.
